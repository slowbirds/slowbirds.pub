Bio

slowbirds

Slowbirds started DJing and Beat Making in Tokyo at the end of 90's.
He moved in Saigon and started the underground bass music party "Base of the Bass" in 2014 and has played at Bass Republic/Error:102/The Obsevatory/The Lighthouse & Vibe Nation Beach Fest/Studio Adventure as well as many other Festival/Parties/Venues across Vietnam.
Co-founder of the online radio and party gang "Mogra Radio" since 2020.

He music spans across Dubby/Deep Bass sounds.
